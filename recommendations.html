MovieBrain - Find the right movie for you!

Personalized settings at your disposal!

Calculating movie suggestions is a complex process that involves a lot of different things. Here are some of the settings which are used by the brain to generate your personal recommended movie list. The sliders indicate the importance of each of the aspects. Slide them as you like!

Which genres do you want?

A static recommendation list makes no sense. You watch different types of movies in different situations, and so here you can filter your recommendation list to only contain (or not contain) movies of specific genres. Simply drag and drop the genre buttons on the exclude or the include panel.

Exclude Genres

Drag and drop genres you do not want in your recommendation list

Include Genres

Drag and drop genres that must be included in your recommendation list

Your movie suggestions

The brain learns from your IMDb ratings to find movies you may like. But guess what, the brain is interactive! Improve the quality of your movie suggestions by adjusting the settings and filters. Make sure you rate at least 5 movies on IMDb (more is better) and your ratings are public, the brain takes care of the rest. Have fun and leave some feedback to support Simon's research.
